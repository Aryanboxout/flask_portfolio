Soccer Facts

Soccer/Football is a sport that was originally created in China but then was refurbished and changed in England. Since then, soccer has spread throughout the world and become the worlds largest sport with over 4 billion people watching and playing the sport. It is mainly popular in Europe and South America which make up the bulkload of the supporters. However, North America and Africa are quickly catching on to the wave and soccer is growing rapidly in these continents.

Basketball Facts

Basketball is becoming one of the quickest growing sports in the world. After originating in USA, the sport is quickly spreading to both Europe and Asia. Many top players in the NBA are from many different locations around the world. Currently basketball isn't ranked too highly in the world in terms of popularity but that is quick to change very soon as the sport continues growing.
